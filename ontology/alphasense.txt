SYM5272 opc_binVolumes: OPC_BIN_VOLUMES, Bin volumes, micrometer3, The volume ascribed to each particle in that bin in firmware

SYM5275 opc_gsc: OPC_GSC, Gain scaling coefficient (GSC), , A global factor to normalise between units. Normally 1.0.

SYM5279 opc_tofToSfrFactor: OPC_TOF_TO_SFR_FACTOR, ToF to SFT factor, , Time of Flight to Sample Flow Rate conversion factor
