SYM1323 i2cWriteReg: I2C_WRITE_REG, I2C write register, , Write a register over raw I2C interface

SYM1332 pinSet: MCU_PIN_SET_DIGITAL, MCU pin set digital output, , Manual control of hardware pin

SYM1333 pinGet: MCU_PIN_GET_DIGITAL, Get value of digital input or output, ,

######## ANALOG IN (ADC)  ########

SYM1334 pinVoltage: MCU_PIN_GET_VOLTAGE, Get voltage level of analog input, volt,

SYM1335 pinV: PIN_VOLTAGES, Pin voltages, volt, Map from pin number to sampled pin voltage

SYM1336 pinVMax: PIN_VOLTAGES_MAX, Max voltages, volt, Map from pin number to highest voltage that can be measured at that pin

SYM1337 pinVoltageSetPoint: MCU_PIN_SET_VOLTAGE_POINT, Calibrate present voltage, , Calibrate voltage measurement on a pin by registering the present true voltage

SYM1338 adcCalib: ADC_CALIBRATE, Calibrate ADC circuit, , Do internal calibration of the ADC circuitry (without external reference)
